About

Sumire Kudo is a painter who shows social messages through her artwork.
Born in Japan, she studied abroad to learn fine arts at various art studios in Los Angeles and worked as a graphic designer with electronic music brands such as Akai professional, RME, M-Audio, Numark, Rane, Denon DJ, and Alesis. She later added freelance illustrator to her artistic resume; providing illustrations for CDs. She joined the Master of Fine Arts program at Laguna College of Art and Design later in her career.
